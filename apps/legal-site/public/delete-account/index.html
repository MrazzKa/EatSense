Delete your EatSense account

This page explains how to request deletion of your EatSense account (developed by EatSense) and the personal data associated with it.

Option 1 — Delete in the app (instant)

  1. Open the EatSense app and sign in.
  2. Go to the Profile tab.
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm. Your account and all associated data are permanently deleted immediately.

Before deleting, you can export a copy of your data via Profile → Export My Data.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, email us from the email address registered to your account:

  • To: privacy@eatsense.ch
  • Subject: Delete my account

We verify the request and delete your account within 30 days, then confirm by email.

What data is deleted

The following are permanently deleted: your account and login credentials, profile (name, email, age, height, weight), health & nutrition data (allergies, conditions, medications, biomarkers), food logs and photos, community posts and messages, expert-consultation history, and notification tokens.

What may be retained

We keep a limited set of records only where required by law — for example transaction/billing records for accounting and tax purposes — for the legally mandated period. Such records are stored separately and are not used for any other purpose. Residual copies in encrypted backups are purged within 90 days.

Note: deleting your account does not cancel an active paid subscription. Manage or cancel subscriptions in Google Play → Subscriptions (or the App Store) separately.

Questions? Contact privacy@eatsense.ch. See also our Privacy Policy.
