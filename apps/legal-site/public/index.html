FAQ

Questions, answered

Is EatSense free?
Yes. You can scan 3 meals a day for free, forever. PRO unlocks unlimited scans and advanced features.
How accurate is the AI analysis?
Our AI estimates calories and macros from a single photo with high accuracy, and you can always fine-tune portions.
Which languages are supported?
EatSense is available in English, German, French, Russian, Kazakh and Spanish.
Is my data private?
Absolutely. We're Swiss-built and GDPR-compliant. Your data is yours — you control exactly what you share.
Can I work with a real nutritionist?
Yes. The Expert Marketplace connects you with certified professionals who can view the data you choose to share.
Is it available on Android and iPhone?
Yes — EatSense is on the App Store for iPhone and on Google Play for Android.
