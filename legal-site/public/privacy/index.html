1. Introduction

Welcome to EatSense. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

EatSense is an AI-powered nutrition tracking application that allows you to photograph food for nutritional analysis, track your meals, monitor biomarkers, and connect with nutrition professionals.

By using our Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

6. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on servers in the European Union (Amsterdam, Netherlands) provided by Railway.

Security measures include:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS/SSL)
  • Secure password hashing
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security assessments

7. Data Retention

We retain your data while your account is active. When you delete your account, all data is permanently deleted immediately.

8. International Data Transfers

Some data is processed in the United States (OpenAI, Expo). We ensure compliance through Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs).

9. Your Rights (GDPR & Swiss FADP)

  • Access: Download your data via "Export My Data" in Settings
  • Rectification: Update your information in the app
  • Erasure: Delete your account via "Delete Account" in Settings
  • Portability: Export data in JSON/CSV format
  • Withdraw Consent: At any time for optional features
  • Lodge Complaint: Contact FDPIC (Switzerland) or your local EU authority

10. Anonymized Data

We may create anonymized, aggregated data for research, improving AI models, and business partnerships. This data cannot identify you personally. You can opt out by contacting us.

11. Children's Privacy

Our Service is for users 18 years or older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 18.
